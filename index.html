Hello I’m Carlo


With 3 years of experience as a Web Designer, I have developed user-friendly and visually engaging web pages for e-commerce platforms such as Rakuten and Yahoo. My work goes beyond layout design—I create compelling visuals for landing pages, perform photo manipulation, craft marketing materials, and support internal design processes, combining creativity with functionality to deliver engaging digital experiences.


Over time, I have expanded my expertise to designing interactive prototypes and wireframes using tools like Adobe XD and Figma, translating concepts into intuitive, solution-driven interfaces that enhance usability and connect users effectively with brands.
